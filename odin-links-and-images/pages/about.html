This page covers common image formats

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos -

  • they are designed for hardling large color palettes without increasing the file size too much
  • i.e makes them great for photos with lots of gradients in them. But don't allow for transparent pixels.

GIFs

GIFs are good for animations.

  • The pesty trade-off is that they are limited in their color palette
  • transparent are a binary option, therefore you cannot have a semi-opaque pixel

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons.

  • Generally bigger than jpg files
  • deal with opacity fine and don't have color palette limiations

SVGs

SVGs are amazing. Use them wherever you can.

  • vector-based graphics
  • one potential issue - if images contain lots of text then has a big impact on file size
